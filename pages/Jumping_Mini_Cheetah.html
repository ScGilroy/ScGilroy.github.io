While forward jumping focused on an optimization pattern on quadrupedal robots, it lacked the implementation on hardware. This page reformulates the problem of forward jumping and navigation of an enviroment and implements everything learning on the MIT Mini Cheetah with results pending publication for IEEE CASE 2021.
